About Me

Who Am I?

I focus on creating meaningful experiences through design. I care about details and consider the design of affordances to be crucial in effective, intuitive interfaces. I have good experience of 9 years in UX Design out of 19 years overall experience in IT Industry working for customers and partners. I conduct user research, implement data-driven and user-oriented UX solutions.

I take ideas from concept to wireframe and wireframe to prototype. Apart from my projects I am leading a team which is providing UI/UX Solutions for Clients. I have good experience of 5 years in Team Leading.
